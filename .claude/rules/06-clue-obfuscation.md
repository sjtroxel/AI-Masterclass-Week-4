# Rule: Clue Obfuscation Standard

All clue text added to `server/data/events.json` or generated by the EventGenerator must conform to The Chronicler's obfuscation protocol:

- No proper nouns — no city names, country names, person names, or monument names.
- No geographic terms that directly identify a location (e.g., "in France," "near the River Thames").
- The clue must remain solvable — contextual, era-based, and topical hints are permitted.

Before finalizing any clue, re-read it and confirm the location cannot be trivially identified from the text alone.
